The Best Movies According to Mishael

My top 3 best movies.

Ozark

Ozark is a crime drama series that follows a financial planner who relocates his family to the Ozarks after a money-laundering scheme goes wrong. The show is known for its intense plot, complex characters, and stunning cinematography.

Spartacus

Spartacus is a historical drama series that tells the story of the gladiator Spartacus and his rebellion against the Roman Republic. The show is known for its graphic violence, strong character development, and intricate political intrigue.

Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is a South Korean webtoon series that follows the story of Sung Jin-Woo, a weak hunter who gains the ability to level up and become stronger. The series is known for its stunning artwork, engaging plot, and character development.
